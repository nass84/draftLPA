How to Apply for a Financial Lasting Power of Attorney

This service will help you create and register a Financial Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA). It takes about 30 minutes to complete, and you can save your progress at the end of each step and return to it later.

  1. Step 1: Enter the donor’s details

    Provide the full name, date of birth, and address of the donor (the person making the LPA).

  2. Step 2: Choose attorneys

    Add one or more attorneys who will manage financial decisions on behalf of the donor.

  3. Step 3: Confirm the certificate provider

    Choose someone to confirm that the donor understands the LPA and is making it willingly.

  4. Step 4: Provide witness signatures

    Arrange for the donor and attorneys to sign the LPA in the presence of witnesses.

  5. Step 5: Print and send to the Office of the Public Guardian

    Check all the details, pay the registration fee, and submit the LPA application to the Office of the Public Guardian.

Start now

Help applying online

The Office of the Public Guardian can guide you through the online process and answer questions. This service may cost extra. Visit GOV.UK for details.

Paper forms

To apply by post, download forms from GOV.UK or call 0300 456 0300. Send completed forms to the Office of the Public Guardian. Postal applications take longer; pay by cheque, debit, or credit card.
