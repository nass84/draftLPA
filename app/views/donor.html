Add donor details

The donor appoints trusted people (attorneys) to make decisions on their behalf. You’ll need to provide:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Address

The donor's role

The donor decides how their Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA) will work. This includes:

  • Choosing who will act as their attorneys
  • Specifying what decisions their attorneys can make

The donor’s choices ensure the LPA meets their needs. Learn more about the donor's responsibilities.

If the donor cannot sign

If the donor is unable to sign or make a mark, someone else can sign on their behalf. This will be recorded on an additional sheet added to the LPA.
