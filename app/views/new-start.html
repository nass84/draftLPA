Create a Lasting Power of Attorney for Financial Decisions

A Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA) lets you choose trusted people (your attorneys) to manage your finances if you’re unable to.

You can choose family, friends, or professionals to act on your behalf.

What your attorneys can do

  • Manage your bank and savings accounts
  • Pay bills and handle expenses
  • Make or sell investments
  • Buy or sell property, including your home

Who can make an LPA?

You (the donor) can create an LPA if you:

  • Are 18 or over
  • Have the mental capacity to make decisions when you set it up

What you’ll need

  • Your full name, address, and date of birth
  • Details of your attorneys and any replacement attorneys
  • A certificate provider to confirm your understanding
  • A witness to sign the form
  • £82 for the registration fee (or proof of fee reduction eligibility)

It takes about 15 minutes to complete your application online.
